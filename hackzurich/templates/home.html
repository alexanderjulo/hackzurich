Migrozept

Ever saw a product in a store that you wanted to eat but had no idea what to cook with it? That is in the past now!

With migrozept you can scan any interesting item in a migros store and find out what amazing things you could cook with it!

Can't find the code or don't have the product nearby right now? Just search for the Name and we will present you with matching results.
